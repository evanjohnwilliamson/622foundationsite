Establishing 'The 622 Foundation'

Mission Statement

To establish the foundational elements, the core essentials, to foster core Pike values and enable the best Scholars, Leaders, Athletes and Gentlemen.

Goals & Objectives

Provide mentorship, guidance and financial support in order to help young men develop and grow through their undergrad experience.

Managed under the guidance of the Alumni Advisory Board (AAB), 'The 622 Foundation' will support the Active Chapter - Iota Omega – of Pi Kappa Alpha by harnessing the power of the Alumni Network.

Our Areas of Focus

Professionally:

  • Lead networking opportunities, lunch & learns, virtually or in person.
  • Enable a discussion for undergrads to learn more about their future career path by meeting with Alumni in their field.
  • Explore and unlock internship opportunities for undergrads.

Personally:

Mentor young men through the trials and challenges that present themselves through the University experience, offering guidance through the transition to post University life.

Financially:

Elevate the potential of the chapter through financial support for core chapter elements – paddle repair & replacement, composite replacement, intramural jerseys, International Fraternity events, scholarships and general support around the Chapter house.

Chapter House Ownership:

Establish a path to taking an equity stake and eventual ownership of the Chapter House.
